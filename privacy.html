Capstone Ready Privacy Policy

Version: 1.0 | Effective date: 22 February 2026

Capstone Ready is provided by Capstone Ready (Australia). Contact: capstoneready@outlook.com.

1. Scope

This policy explains how personal information is handled in Capstone Ready, consistent with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988.

2. Information we collect

  • Apple Sign-In identifier and profile data provided by Apple.
  • Learning data such as quiz attempts, scores, progress, readiness, and analytics records.
  • Subscription and entitlement status from Apple StoreKit.
  • Support details you provide when contacting us.

3. How we collect information

  • Directly from you when signing in, using app features, or contacting support.
  • Automatically through app usage needed for core functionality.
  • From Apple services used for billing and subscription verification.

4. Why we use information

  • Provide account access and app features.
  • Track and present progress and readiness insights.
  • Process subscriptions, purchases, and restores.
  • Provide support and improve app reliability.

5. Storage and security

We use reasonable technical and organisational controls to protect information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Sensitive local auth/session data may be stored using platform-secure storage where applicable.

6. Disclosure and sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be disclosed to service providers necessary to operate the app (for example Apple services) or where required by law.

7. Overseas disclosure

Some providers may process limited data outside Australia. Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate handling and protection.

8. Access and correction

You can request access to or correction of personal information by contacting capstoneready@outlook.com.

9. Complaints

If you believe your privacy has been mishandled, contact capstoneready@outlook.com. We will review and respond within a reasonable timeframe.

10. Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for app operations, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and legitimate business purposes.

11. Updates to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version date will always be shown on this page.
