Include in your support request

  • Device model and iOS/iPadOS version
  • App version/build number
  • Short issue summary and screenshot where possible
  • Whether the issue is sign-in, premium, quiz/progress, or profiling related

Common support scope

  • Sign in with Apple and session issues
  • Google/Facebook/email sign-in setup and login issues
  • Face ID / Touch ID and periodic presence verification issues
  • In-app purchase and Restore Purchases issues
  • Premium entitlement and expiry state checks
  • Notification center and messaging sync issues
  • Tutorial booking and reminder issues
  • Weekly profile export/distribution troubleshooting

Premium validity

Premium currently includes a 10-week non-renewing pass. Access expires at end of term unless purchased again.

Premium vs RTO Subscription

Learner premium and any future RTO portal subscription are separate products with separate billing and access rights.
