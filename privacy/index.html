Privacy Policy

CapstoneReady

Version 1.2 | Effective date: 25 February 2026

CapstoneReady is provided by Spark's Capstone Ready (Australia). Contact: capstoneready@outlook.com. Website: www.capstoneready.au. Creator: Aaron Roper (Surfline Electrical).

1) Scope

This policy explains how personal information is handled in CapstoneReady in an Australian context and aligned to the Australian Privacy Principles.

2) Information We Collect

  • Apple Sign-In identifiers and profile fields provided by Apple.
  • Google/Facebook/email sign-in identifiers where those providers are used.
  • Learning progress records, including quiz attempts, scores, module completion, and readiness status.
  • Weekly profiling records entered by users, including recipient emails and supervisor signature data where provided.
  • Premium entitlement status and transaction state from Apple StoreKit.
  • Support details supplied in support requests.

3) How We Collect Information

  • Directly from users when signing in, using quiz/profiling features, or contacting support.
  • Automatically from app interactions required for feature operation.
  • From Apple, Google, Facebook, and configured identity services for authentication and purchase entitlement checks.

4) Why We Use Information

  • Provide account access and core app functionality.
  • Track learning progress and readiness.
  • Deliver profile export/distribution workflows to user-selected recipients.
  • Verify, activate, and restore premium entitlement.
  • Maintain premium entitlement windows (10 weeks per in-app purchase).
  • Apply security controls and maintain app stability/support.

5) Storage and Security

Progress and profiling records are stored locally on-device. Sensitive local payloads are encrypted at rest using platform security features (including Keychain-backed keys where available). Optional biometric app lock can be enabled by users.

6) Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with Apple, Google, Facebook, and essential service components needed to run app features, or where required by law.

7) Overseas Disclosure

Some services may process limited information outside Australia. Where used, we seek providers with appropriate safeguards.

8) Access and Correction

Requests for access or correction can be sent to capstoneready@outlook.com.

9) Complaints

If you have a privacy complaint, contact capstoneready@outlook.com with details. We will investigate and respond within a reasonable timeframe.

10) Retention

Information is retained only while required for app operation, legal obligations, or support. Users can reset local app data from in-app settings.

11) Updates

We may update this policy. The current version and effective date are shown on this page.
