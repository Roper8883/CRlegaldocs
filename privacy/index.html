Compliance notice: Spark's Capstone Ready is not a Registered Training Organisation (RTO). CapstoneReady is used for preparation and training support only.

1) Scope

This policy explains how personal information is handled in CapstoneReady in an Australian context and aligned to the Australian Privacy Principles.

2) Information We Collect

  • Apple Sign-In identifiers and profile fields provided by Apple.
  • Google/Facebook/email sign-in identifiers where those providers are used.
  • Learning progress records, including quiz attempts, scores, module completion, and readiness status.
  • Weekly profiling records entered by users, including recipient emails and supervisor signature data where provided.
  • Notification center and messaging metadata used for inbox sync, signature workflow, and tutorial reminders.
  • Premium entitlement status and transaction state from Apple StoreKit.
  • Tutorial booking data (for example topic, tutor details, session time, and status events).
  • Support details supplied in support requests.

3) How We Collect Information

  • Directly from users when signing in, using quiz/profiling features, or contacting support.
  • Automatically from app interactions required for feature operation.
  • From Apple, Google, Facebook, and configured identity services for authentication and purchase entitlement checks.

4) Why We Use Information

  • Provide account access and core app functionality.
  • Track learning progress and readiness.
  • Deliver profile export/distribution workflows to user-selected recipients.
  • Verify, activate, and restore premium entitlement.
  • Maintain premium entitlement windows for purchased premium access (currently a non-renewing 10-week pass).
  • Support notifications and workflow events across profiling, messaging, and tutorial booking.
  • Apply security controls and maintain app stability/support.

5) Storage and Security

Progress and profiling records are stored locally on-device. Sensitive local payloads are encrypted at rest using platform security features (including Keychain-backed keys where available). During signed-in sessions, periodic device presence verification checks are enforced (biometric where available).

6) Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with Apple, Google, Facebook, and essential service components needed to run app features, or where required by law.

7) Overseas Disclosure

Some services may process limited information outside Australia. Where used, we seek providers with appropriate safeguards.

8) Third-Party Links

The app may open external services such as YouTube links for tutorial content. Third-party platforms operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

9) Access and Correction

Requests for access or correction can be sent to capstoneready@outlook.com.

You can also delete your app account state and local app data in-app via Settings -> Data -> Delete Account & Data.

Deleting app account data does not delete your Apple/Google/Facebook provider account, which remains managed by that provider.

10) Premium and RTO Subscription Separation

The learner premium pass and any future RTO portal subscription are separate products. A learner premium purchase does not grant RTO portal access, and an RTO portal subscription does not grant learner premium entitlements.

11) Complaints

If you have a privacy complaint, contact capstoneready@outlook.com with details. We will investigate and respond within a reasonable timeframe.

12) Retention

Information is retained only while required for app operation, legal obligations, or support. Users can reset local app data from in-app settings or use the in-app account deletion control.

13) Updates

We may update this policy. The current version and effective date are shown on this page.
