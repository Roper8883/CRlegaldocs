Capstone Ready Support

Help with sign-in, subscriptions, and app issues.

Contact

  • Email: capstoneready@outlook.com
  • App: Capstone Ready
  • Region: Australia

What to include in your request

  • Device model and iOS/iPadOS/macOS version
  • App version and build number
  • A short description of the issue and screenshot (if available)

Support scope

  • Sign in with Apple issues
  • Face ID / app lock issues
  • Purchase, restore, and premium entitlement issues
  • Question content, exam flow, and progress sync issues

Premium purchase validity

Premium access from in-app purchase is valid for 10 weeks from the date of purchase. After the 10-week period ends, Premium access expires unless purchased again.
